Pricing Details

My prices are negotiable! Due to the variety of products I'm able to create, the prices can vary drastically. If you're questioning a price about commissioning me, the best course of action is to contact me through my email, Discord, or by Twitter. Although complexity does play a major part in what I charge, below is some details about my minimum rates.

I accept payments in USD ($) and Robux (R$). If the latter method (R$) is chosen, group funds are heavily preferred, and the funds should already be deposited in the group to reduce wait-time.

Payment Methods

  • Robux (R$)

These are rough estimates of what I charge. I don't tend to go lower than these prices below, but complexity/workload will affect these prices.

Animation

  • 7k R$ | $25 USD: Cyclic Animation
  • 10k R$ | $35 USD: Action Animation

Price for designing, creating, and importing the animation. Clients will receive the .blend file alongside a .rbxm containing a rig with the animation.

Programming

  • 45k R$ | $80 USD: Custom System

Price for planning, flowcharting, and programming a system. Clients will receive the .RBXL file with systems, alongside the flowchart.

Rigging

  • 8k R$ | $28 USD: Motor6D Rig, no deformation
  • 15k R$ | $50 USD: Motor6D + Bone Rig, deformation

Price for rigging, and prettifying the rig for animators. Clients will receive the animatable rig, alongside a .blend file with the rig for Blender animators.
